# ZimProperty - Phase 1: Strategy & Architecture

## 1. Product Requirements Document (PRD)
**Problem Statement:** Finding affordable and reliable housing in Zimbabwe is fragmented. Students struggle to find boarding near universities, and the general market lacks a consolidated, trustworthy platform with deep local context (e.g., borehole/solar status).

**User Personas:**
- **Tariro (Student):** 19, searching for safe boarding near UZ. Uses Android phone. Needs WiFi and proximity.
- **Farai (Middle-class Professional):** 34, looking to buy a first home in Harare. Needs borehole and solar details.
- **Mrs. Moyo (Landlord):** 55, wants to list a cottage in Bulawayo. Needs simple WhatsApp-based lead management.

**Competitive Analysis:**
- **Property24 Zim:** Strong catalog, but feels generic/international.
- **Classifieds.co.zw:** High traffic, but cluttered and full of low-quality listings.
- **ZimProperty Edge:** Dedicated student boarding filters and local infrastructure focus (Borehole/Solar).
